AI Advisory

AI that surfaces. Humans that decide.

LuxGov's AI advisory layer surfaces information — it does not make compliance decisions. Every verdict is computed by the governance engine and confirmed by an authorised human role.

The system is architecturally designed so that removing AI entirely does not change a single compliance outcome. AI is a productivity tool — not an authority.

About LuxGov

Founded on the belief that compliance should be governed, not guessed.

LuxGov was built to solve a structural problem in the Australian construction industry: compliance is managed through documents, not governance. Approvals are delayed, risks are hidden, and authority is unclear.

Our platform replaces ad-hoc compliance workflows with a structured governance engine — deterministic, auditable, and always human-authorised.
