The Process

From submission to handover.

A structured five-phase workflow that guides your project from initial review all the way through to CCC submission.

01
Preliminary Review

Submit project details and receive a structured compliance checklist tailored to your building class and jurisdiction.

02
ITP & Schedule Setup

Define your Inspection & Test Plan. Assign responsible parties, set inspection frequencies, and link to evidence requirements.

03
Evidence Collection

Upload reports, photos, test certificates, and drawings to structured libraries. Version-controlled and fully auditable.

04
Phase Gate Sign-Off

Gatekeepers review and approve each phase. Automated audit trails capture every decision with timestamps.

05
Handover & CCC

Generate a complete handover package. Submit with confidence knowing every compliance obligation is documented and signed off.
