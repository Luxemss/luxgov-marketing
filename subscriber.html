Evidence-first compliance tools designed for real projects — not promises. This page explains what access as a LuxGov Subscriber means in practice.

Who this is for

Built for professionals on regulated construction projects

LuxGov Subscribers are the people doing the compliance work — uploading evidence, reviewing findings, and preparing documentation for certification. If your role involves supplying evidence, identifying gaps, or getting a project ready for submission, LuxGov is built for you.

Subscriber access

What you can do as a Subscriber

As a LuxGov Subscriber, you have access to the full evidence and audit workflow for your assigned projects. All outputs are evidence-based and audit-ready.

  • Upload drawings and supporting evidence (PDF, DWG, IFC, XLSX)
  • Run regulatory and drawing checks against applicable jurisdictional rules
  • See which compliance areas have evidence — and which don't
  • Review structured gap findings (what's missing, incomplete, or unclear)
  • Generate narrative audit reports in plain language for teams and certifiers
  • Export reports to PDF and DOCX for offline review and submission
Day-to-day value

How LuxGov supports your work

LuxGov is designed to help professionals spend less time chasing gaps and more time resolving them. It brings structure to evidence review without replacing the professional judgement behind it.

  • ✓
    Identify missing or weak evidence early — before it becomes a submission problem
  • ✓
    Reduce back-and-forth with certifiers by preparing cleaner, more complete submissions
  • ✓
    Keep audit trails organised, traceable, and defensible at any stage
  • ✓
    Avoid last-minute surprises by monitoring evidence coverage throughout the project
  • ✓
    Generate structured reports your team and certifiers can read without interpretation
LuxGov does not replace professional judgement. It supports it — by making the evidence picture clear, structured, and traceable at every stage.
Governance & trust

The same rules apply to every Subscriber

LuxGov is designed so that compliance integrity cannot be bypassed or adjusted by any user, plan, or configuration. These principles are fixed across the platform.

What LuxGov never does
  • Declare a project compliant
  • Approve or certify any work
  • Weaken checks for higher-tier plans
  • Override evidence requirements
What stays the same for everyone
  • Identical evidence standards
  • Identical scoring thresholds
  • Identical gap-finding logic
  • Non-determination safeguards
Plans change scale, automation, and integration — not outcomes. LuxGov scales capacity and speed. It never scales truth.
Your role in the organisation

How Subscribers fit into a LuxGov organisation

Subscribers are assigned to projects by a Buyer — the person in your organisation who manages project access and users. Billing is handled separately by a Purchaser. As a Subscriber, your focus is on the compliance work itself, not account management.
