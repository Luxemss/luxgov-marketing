A
Evidence Gap Narration

Reads an evidence manifest and narrates observable coverage and gaps in plain language — scoped to what is explicitly present in the provided data.

B
Document Coherence Review

Reads a document against its template schema and lists observable structural gaps or unpopulated placeholders — without assessing readiness or approval status.

C
Audit Trail Readability Digest

Reads a decrypted audit log export and summarises observable activity patterns and BLOCKED events as logged — without characterising them as violations.

What it does
  • — Reads evidence manifests and describes observable coverage
  • — Identifies structural gaps and unpopulated placeholders in documents
  • — Narrates audit log activity patterns in plain language
  • — Produces timestamped, session-logged advisory artefacts
  • — Operates within a version-locked, operator-assembled constitutional prompt
What it does NOT do
  • — Determine, approve, reject, or advance any governance matter
  • — Produce compliance verdicts or legal interpretations
  • — Characterise audit events as violations or non-compliance
  • — Recommend escalation, remediation, or phase transitions
  • — Replace any human authority in the governance hierarchy

Governance note.  All Mission AI Advisory output is an Advisory Artefact for human consideration only and is non-binding. The advisory boundary is structurally enforced at the prompt architecture layer — it does not depend on user discipline. Human governance authority is retained in full at all times.
